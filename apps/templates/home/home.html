About RingleDingle

RingleDingle is a fun and friendly e-greeting card service that uses advanced AI technology to generate personalized poems for your loved ones. Choose from a variety of celebrity voices, such as Johnny Cash and Betty White, to narrate the poem in an audio file with a backing track. An image of the poem, as interpreted by DALLE, will also be included in the card.

How it Works

  1. Enter your email and information about the recipient of the RingleDingle.
  2. Select a celebrity voice to narrate your poem.
  3. Our AI technology will generate a personalized poem that describes the recipient.
  4. Choose the poem you like, and our system will generate the full card and/or email, complete with an audio file, AI narration, and an image of the poem as interpreted by DALLE.
